Minyan Times

Fixed — A specific time that doesn't change from week to week (e.g., 7:00 AM).

Dynamic — Calculated relative to a halachic time (zman). Choose the zman and an offset in minutes. For example, Netz −20 min sets the minyan 20 minutes before sunrise.

Rounded — Like Dynamic, but the result is rounded down to the nearest 5-minute interval. Useful for Mincha b'zman so the time stays consistent across the week.

No Minyan — No service on this day or occasion.
